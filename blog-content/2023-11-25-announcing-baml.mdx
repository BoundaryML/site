Announcing the first public launch of BAML. BAML is a lightweight programming language to help perform structured prompting in a typesafe way. It is designed to be a typesafe interface to LLMs, with built-in testing, guardrails and observability.

## If you want to get started:

1. [Give us a star](https://github.com/gloohq/baml)
2. [Join our Discord](https://discord.gg/BTNBeXGuaS)
3. [Install BAML](https://docs.boundaryml.com/v3/home/installation) (< 1 minute)
4. Read our tutorials: https://docs.boundaryml.com/v3/guides/hello_world/level0
5. Setup a call - [Calendly](https://calendly.com/boundaryml/meeting-with-founders?back=1&month=2023-12)
